Dems Da Rules!!!

Click into the minefield to expose free space.
The numbers show how many bombs are adjacent to that square.
Use your math skills and powers of deduction to
identify where the bombs live.
Right-click to place a flag where you think a bomb may be.
A flag will prevent you from accidentally sploding a bomb.
The game ends when you have opened all empty squares or
have tripped a bomb.
In the game of Mine Sweepaaaa you win...
or you die.
